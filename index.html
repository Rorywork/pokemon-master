Welcome to Pokemon Master the ultimate Pokemon guessing game.

You will be given the back of a Gen 1 Pokemon and up to three clues before having to pick from five different Pokemon.

If you get the answer right you gain a point and if you get it wrong the game will end. You press the A button on the GameBoy or A on the keyboard to advance through the clues then when you are ready to guess, press the B button or B on your keyboard. After this, you can select the Pokemon using the gamepad arrow keys or the arrow keys on your keyboard, then press A to select your answer..
